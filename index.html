About Me

Hi, I am Vlad Alin Paduraru and I am a freelance Unity developer from Bolzano, Italy.

I studied Computer Science and Engineering at the Free University of Bolzano and graduated from the bachelor in 2022.

Videogames have always been my passion so I decided to focus on learning all the aspects of Game Development, ranging from programming to modelling to animations, shaders and vfx.

In 2017 I attended a course about Virtual Reality development where I learnt how to use Unity and Unreal Engine 4. Due to different requests to develop WebGL applications in the past I settled on Unity.

In my free time I like to study new technologies, as well as creating 3D models for fun. My goal is to learn something new every day.
